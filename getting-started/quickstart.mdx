This quickstart guide shows you how to create your first video and article in Clueso in minutes.

After completing this guide, you will have a great video and documentation article that you can share.

<Info>
  **Prerequisite**: Before you begin, [create an account](https://web.clueso.io) and complete onboarding.
</Info>

## Getting started

After you complete the onboarding process, you'll find yourself on the [Clueso home page](https://web.clueso.io).

At the top of the home page, you'll see three options to create a new video:

1. Record screen
2. Upload a video
3. Upload a slide deck

## Install the chrome extension

In this guide, we'll record our screen to capture a rough screen recording, and turn that rough recording into a polished video.

To capture recordings, you'll need to install the Clueso chrome extension.

1. Head to the Clueso extension on the [chrome web store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/clueso-extension/hnfgakkbiefcdbkonflgfkadncclmpme?hl=en-GB&authuser=0) then click **Add to Chrome** \> **Add extension**.
2. Pin the extension by clicking on the **puzzle icon** in your browser, then click on the **pin icon**.

   ![00524a88-4a98-4b3d-ad54-cb947e25deec.gif](/images/00524a88-4a98-4b3d-ad54-cb947e25deec.gif)

   The Clueso Chrome extension is your one-stop solution to record content in Clueso.

## Capture a screen recording

With the Clueso extension now installed, let's start a recording.

There are two ways to initiate a recording on Clueso. Either click on the Clueso extension icon, or click on the _Record Screen_ from the home screen.

Follow the on-screen instructions to capture a recording on any product of your choice. Walk through the process you're following with your voice. There's no need to be perfect here. Just go through the flow roughly.

## Edit your video 

Once you finish your recording, you'll be taken into the video editor. 

Check out this guide for a more in-depth tutorial on editing your first video. 

## Edit your article 

Now, head over to the article tab. Here, you can edit your article.
